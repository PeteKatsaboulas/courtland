 About Us

Knoxville is home, and we’re here to welcome you.

The Courtland Group is more than a just a real estate development company. We are an inspired team of developers, builders, and relationship specialists. Our passion for this city and its people is evident in every building we touch and every connection we make.

As developers, we recognize potential and exceed expectations. Our aim is to keep Knoxville’s history alive by preserving and revitalizing old buildings. The Courtland Group believes each and every building tells a story and has the potential to create new ones. Let us help you begin your story.

As builders, we pride ourselves on quality work – building commercial spaces and homes made to last. The Courtland Group’s architectural planning continually sets new standards for Knoxville’s community. We preserve the original charm of each building we touch, whilst being aware of the modern lifestyle our tenants desire.

As tenant relationship specialists, we go above and beyond what’s expected in order to make you feel safe and listened to. We respond quickly and personally to each request no matter how big or small it may be. Our goal is to help you feel at home.
