Patricia Nash

Emerging as a leading, designer leather handbag and accessory brand which recently launched footwear, Patricia Nash has moved into its new Headquarters near downtown Knoxville. The newly renovated industrial building located off of 6th Avenue will house its Flagship Showroom, creative and corporate operations, and E-commerce distribution center. Patricia Nash’s sales team will continue to operate out of Nash’s NYC showroom.
